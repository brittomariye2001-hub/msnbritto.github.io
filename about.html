The Story

I didn't study design.
I studied meaning.

I'm Britto MSN — a multidisciplinary creative designer operating at the intersection of fine art theory and commercial design practice. My work spans brand identity, graphic design, motion graphics, video editing, and rebranding — disciplines most creatives treat as separate, that I treat as one unified language.

I hold an MFA in Fine Arts — a credential that fundamentally shapes how I approach every brief. Most designers learn tools. I learned principles. The difference shows in work that doesn't just look contemporary — it holds up against time, scrutiny, and scale.

Eight years of practice means I've worked across industries, brand scales, and creative challenges. I've built identities from nothing. I've rescued brands that had lost their way. I've translated complex strategic ideas into visual systems that non-designers immediately understand.

I believe the best design work begins not at a screen, but with a question: what does this brand need to make people feel? Everything else — colour, form, motion, type — is the answer to that question.

The Journey

How I got here.

2016

The Beginning

Started as a graphic designer — learning the fundamentals of composition, typography, and visual communication. Built the discipline that every discipline after would depend on.

2018

First Brand System

Delivered the first complete brand identity project. Understood that design at its best is a system, not a series of individual decisions — a principle that has governed every project since.

2020

MFA Completed

Graduated with a Master of Fine Arts — deepening understanding of art history, visual theory, semiotics, and the philosophical foundations of design. Theory became the engine behind the practice.

2021

Expanded into Motion

Added motion graphics and video editing to the practice. The same principles of narrative, rhythm, and composition that govern static design translate directly — and powerfully — to time-based media.

2022

Rebranding Specialisation

Started focusing on rebranding — helping established businesses shed identities that no longer served them. This work is as much strategic therapy as visual design.

2025

Britto MSN Studio

Formalised the practice. 8+ years. 150+ projects delivered. A clear creative point of view and the experience to defend every decision. The work continues to evolve — the standards never lower.
